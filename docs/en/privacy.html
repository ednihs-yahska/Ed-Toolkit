Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 22, 2025

Ed-Toolkit values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our macOS application.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data. All processing is done locally on your device. The app does not connect to external servers, APIs, or third-party services.

2. Offline Functionality

The application works entirely offline. Your text, JSON data, and URLs are processed securely on your Mac and are never transmitted over the internet.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any form of analytics, tracking, or advertising. Your interactions with the app remain entirely private.

4. Permissions

The app does not request access to your files, network, contacts, or any sensitive data. It only uses system resources necessary for local rendering and processing.

5. Security

Since all functionality runs locally and the app does not communicate with external systems, there is no risk of data exposure through network transmission.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted within the app or on our official website.
